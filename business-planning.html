How We Help Business Owners

Director's Pensions

Company pension contributions are one of the most tax-efficient ways to extract profits.

  • Corporation tax relief on contributions
  • No National Insurance for you or your company
  • Annual allowance up to £60,000
  • SSAS and SIPP options for business owners
  • Relevant Life Insurance policies

Business Protection

Protect your business and your partners from the unexpected.

  • Shareholder protection insurance
  • Partnership protection for LLPs
  • Key person insurance for critical employees
  • Loan protection for business debts
  • Cross-option agreements

Exit & Succession Planning

Plan your exit strategy years in advance to maximize value and minimize tax.

  • Business Asset Disposal Relief (formerly Entrepreneurs' Relief)
  • Family succession planning
  • Management buyout (MBO) planning
  • Sale structuring for tax efficiency
  • Post-exit wealth management

Tax-Efficient Remuneration

Structure your director's pay to minimize tax and National Insurance.

  • Pension contributions vs dividends
  • Benefits in kind planning
  • Director's loan accounts
  • Company car vs mileage allowance
